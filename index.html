Gender bias in job hiring refers to the systematic favoritism or discrimination against individuals of a particular gender during the recruitment and selection process.

  • Gendered Language in Job Descriptions
  • Unconscious Bias in Screening and Selection.
  • Organizational Culture and Practices

Addressing gender bias in job hiring requires proactive measures to promote fairness, equality, and diversity in recruitment and selection processes. This may involve implementing strategies such as using gender-neutral language in job postings, conducting blind resume reviews, providing unconscious bias training for hiring teams, establishing diverse interview panels, and fostering inclusive workplace cultures that value and support individuals of all genders.
